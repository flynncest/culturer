The process

Five steps
to zero effort.

Set up once. culturers runs every automation from that point forward — no reminders, no manual orders, no missed birthdays.

01
You add your people
Upload your team via CSV or add employees one by one. Include names, birthdays, dietary needs, and delivery tier. Takes under five minutes for a team of 50.
What we capture
Name, email, birthday, work anniversary
Dietary needs: lactose, nut, gluten, vegan
Delivery address (your office, or per person)
Celebration tier (Birthday / Premium / Prospect)
02
We watch the calendar
culturers scans every employee's birthday and anniversary automatically. You don't need to check a thing. We surface upcoming deliveries in your dashboard and alert you only when something needs attention.
Automated triggers
Birthday detected 7 days in advance
Anniversary alerts with personalised note options
Team celebration scheduling by group
Prospect flow triggers on date or deal stage
03
Smart cake selection
Our algorithm cross-references each person's dietary needs against the cake catalog. No two people get the same flavour. Allergen conflicts are flagged automatically — no surprises at the door.
How we assign
Allergen check across full cake catalog
Variety distribution so every person differs
Manual override always available
Seasonal specials surface automatically
04
We route the order
culturers connects directly to our network of artisan baker partners across Amsterdam, Berlin, London, and beyond. Orders are placed, confirmed, and tracked — all without you touching a thing.
Logistics handled
Order placed with nearest artisan partner
Baked fresh — never frozen or pre-packaged
Personalised message card printed and enclosed
Same-day confirmation email sent to you

The cake arrives. The employee feels seen. That feeling compounds over time — birthdays become expected, anniversaries feel earned. Culture is not built in big moments. It's built in small ones, repeated.

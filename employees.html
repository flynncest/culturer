The process

How it works.
Simpler than you think.

01
You send us a list
Email us a CSV or fill our short survey with names, emails and dates. No HR system connection needed. That is all we need to get started.
02
We prep the month ahead
At the start of each month you get a clear overview of upcoming birthdays and anniversaries. You confirm who gets a gift. We handle everything else.
03
We send the moment
A personalised message and optionally a small gift — delivered to their home or office. Timed right, felt personal, handled by us.
04
They receive something real
A handmade cake. A digital card built around their interests. A voucher for something they actually love. It compounds — every year they feel more known.

You send the list.
We do the rest.

Our commitment

We refused to
automate the part
that matters.

Every cake that arrives at someone's door was made by hand, from scratch, by a person who genuinely cares about their craft — not a factory optimised for volume, not a conveyor belt, not a central hub warehouse.

culturers sits in the gap between convenience and meaning. Technology handles the timing and logistics. Artisans handle the making. The result doesn't feel like a corporate program. It feels like someone thought of you.

That feeling is not accidental. It's engineered — through the curation of our baker network, our ingredient standards, and our insistence that every delivery is worth remembering.

Each culturers baker partner is visited in person before joining our network. We taste their work, review their process, and check that their values align with ours: fresh ingredients, honest craft, zero shortcuts.

From order to door

How a culturers cake
comes to life.

01
Order placed through the platform
culturers triggers the order automatically based on the employee's birthday or the scheduled send date. No manual action required from you.
Routed to the nearest baker partner
Our system selects the closest available partner based on the delivery address, their current capacity, and the cake type requested.
02
Baked fresh on delivery day
The baker receives the order the evening before. They bake fresh that morning. The cake arrives the same day it leaves the oven.
Allergen check before baking
Recipient dietary needs are flagged to the baker automatically. They confirm the recipe is safe before starting.
03
Card printed and enclosed
The personalised message from your dashboard is printed on a culturers card and placed with the cake before it leaves the bakery.
Delivered and confirmed
Delivery is confirmed in your dashboard. The recipient's response logs automatically. Next year's delivery is already pre-scheduled.
